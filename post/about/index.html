来自北京，现为北京正常大学哲学专业本科生，最大的梦想是做个不正常的人。生活常态是面无表情地读文献写论文，在思维中痛苦和快乐。用各种方式探索身体，尤其是舞蹈中的身体实践、生活中的身体实践和理论中的身体的交汇点。喜欢一个人在关了灯的房间里跳舞。

Born in Beijing, Cindy is currently working on her philosophy BA at Beijing Normal University. Her biggest dream is to be an abnormal person. Her day-to-day life is constituted of academic reading and writing without any facial expression, feeling the pain and pleasure inside the mind. She explores the body in various ways, especially the intersection of bodily movement in dance, in everyday life, and the body theory. She enjoys dancing alone in a darkened room.
