曹新笛 CAO Xindi

来自北京，现为北京正常大学哲学专业本科生，研究兴趣是身体现象学。她的创作实践以开放性概念下的舞蹈和剧场为背景，关注不同层面的内与外、介入与静观、表象与实质。她预期的方向既是哲学，也是艺术，同时既不是哲学，也不是艺术。用艺术的方式，同时也是哲学的方式，身体的方式，同时也是思维的方式，探知“我”和“世界”。近期她也在进行一些关于“____ 作为舞蹈评论”及“舞蹈评论作为 ____”的实验。

Born in Beijing, Xindi Cao is currently working on her philosophy BA at Beijing Normal University with a research interest in the phenomenology of body. In her performance practice, she explores the representation and essence, the intervention and contemplation, the inner and outer space at various levels in an expanded understanding of dance and theatre. Her anticipated direction is both philosophy and art, at the same time, neither philosophy nor art. To perceive the self and the world, she jointly adopts the way of philosophy/mind and the way of art/body. She is also experimenting with “____ as dance criticism” and “dance criticism as ____”.
